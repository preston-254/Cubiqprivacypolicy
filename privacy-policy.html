Cubiq Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026
Developer: TechHeart Games

1. Introduction

TechHeart Games (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates Cubiq, a block puzzle game available on the web and mobile devices. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use Cubiq, how it is used, and the choices you have.

By playing Cubiq, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. Information We Collect

Cubiq is designed to work primarily on your device. We collect limited information to provide gameplay, save progress, and optional leaderboard features.

Information stored on your device

Using your browser’s or device’s local storage, Cubiq may save:

  • A randomly generated player ID
  • An optional username you choose (up to 16 characters)
  • Your selected country (country code and display name)
  • Game scores, including Quick Match best score and weekly score history
  • Career mode progress (levels unlocked and completed)
  • Game save data so you can resume a session
  • Leaderboard data, including friends you add and private leagues you create or join
  • App settings (sound and vibration preferences)
  • Whether you have completed the tutorial

This data stays on your device unless you enable cloud leaderboard sync (see below) or clear your browser/app storage.

Information sent to our servers (optional)

Cloud leaderboard features are disabled by default. If enabled by the app operator, Cubiq may sync the following to a secure database hosted by Supabase:

  • Your player ID
  • Your username
  • Your country code
  • Quick Match best score, total score, weekly score, and career level
  • Timestamp of last update

We do not collect passwords, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information through Cubiq.

Information we do not collect

  • Precise GPS location
  • Contacts from your device (friends are added manually in the app)
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Advertising identifiers for targeted ads (Cubiq does not show third-party ads)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide core gameplay and save your progress
  • Display leaderboards, country leagues, and private leagues
  • Rank players by weekly and all-time scores
  • Remember your settings and preferences
  • Improve stability and fix bugs

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Device Features

If you enable vibration in Settings, Cubiq uses your device’s vibration API for haptic feedback during gameplay. No vibration data is stored or transmitted.

If you use the “Invite Friends” feature, Cubiq may open your device’s share sheet or copy an invite message to your clipboard. We do not receive the contents of messages you send.

5. Third-Party Services

Cubiq may rely on the following third parties:

  • Google Fonts — typography may be loaded from Google’s servers when you open the app. Google may receive your IP address and basic device information. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase (optional) — cloud leaderboard storage when enabled by the deployment. See Supabase’s Privacy Policy.
  • Hosting provider — if you access Cubiq over the internet, standard server logs (such as IP address, browser type, and access time) may be recorded by whoever hosts the website.

Cubiq does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in the current version.

6. Cookies

Cubiq does not use cookies for tracking. Progress and preferences are stored using browser local storage or equivalent on-device storage.

7. Children’s Privacy

Cubiq is suitable for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

8. Data Retention

Data stored on your device remains until you clear your browser or app storage, uninstall the app, or reset your device. Cloud leaderboard data, when enabled, is retained for as long as needed to operate leaderboards unless deletion is requested.

9. Your Choices & Rights

You can:

  • Choose whether to set a username and country in your Profile
  • Turn sound and vibration on or off in Settings
  • Leave private leagues at any time
  • Clear local data by clearing site data in your browser or app settings
  • Request deletion of cloud leaderboard data by contacting us (when cloud sync is enabled)

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws (such as access, correction, or deletion). Contact us to exercise those rights.

10. International Users

If cloud features are enabled, your information may be processed on servers located outside your country. By using Cubiq, you consent to such processing where permitted by law.

11. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect information, including secure connections (HTTPS) for web delivery and industry-standard hosting for optional cloud data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective date” at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of Cubiq after changes means you accept the updated policy.
